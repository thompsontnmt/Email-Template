Set Up Auto-Reply

Ensure that you set up an auto-reply email message to let people know you are out of office and will be returning soon!

Secure Your Home and Office

Take home any items you do not wish to leave at the office and tidy your workspace. Ensure your home doors and windows are locked and safegaurded from vulnerabilities.

Keep Your Phone Charged

Many people like to unplug completely, but you never know when you know when you may need to be in contact with someone urgently. Keep electronics charged and closeby - great for navigation, translation, and emergencies!
